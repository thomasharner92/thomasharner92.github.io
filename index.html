About Me

My name is Thomas Harner, and I am currently living in Fairfax, VA. I graduated with a B.S. in Geographic Information Systems from James Madision University in 2015 and a recently obtained my M.S. in GIS and Web Map Programming from the Univeristy of Wisconsin-Madison.

I currently work as a sub-contractor to ESRI as a front-end developer on projects supporting the National Geospatial Intelligence Agency (NGA).

Before that, I worked for 3.5 years for the Army Corps of Engineers creating an Android application to determine Helicopter Landing Zones from terrain meshes.

I enjoy working with open source geospatial software tools, and continue to strive to build up a stronger background as a developer with a geospatial focus.

In my spare time, I enjoy running, kayaking, and hiking around Northern Virginia and the Shenandoah Valley. My wife and I are close followers of local wine and craft breweries, having visited more than 50 vineyards throughout the state of Virgina.
